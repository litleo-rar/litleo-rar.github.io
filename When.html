Day 1 Nocturnal Records

  • by Admin
  • 2 Comments

Today we bought our domain name for $0.99. It’s only good for a year so we’re gonna try to make it count.

I’m other news, Nathan’s tiny house almost has a sink and Britt‘s papers are organized. Coding is hard, so thank you to all those who spent hair splitting hours so that I can share with you our daily (jk weekly or whenevs) successes and failures Trying the knock-off volcano vaporizer with some freshly trimmed purple chem while listening to the Beatles. Here at Nocturnal Records we vow to bring to you the most genuine... no promises to be good or any better than regular old decent, but it’ll at least be authentic. Imagine those late, wee hours of the night when you get a sudden spurt of energy or an idea pops into your head and you have to write it down. Nocturnal records is based on that foundation. Middle of the night, semi conscious, jittery inspiration that keeps us running on Dunkin or whatever other last minute coffee decisions we make in the morning.

Thank you Go Daddy. Fuck Trump. Fuck Coronavirus. BLM. Ok I think that covers it all.

Until next time!
